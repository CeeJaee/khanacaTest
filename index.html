Recipe #1: Grilled Cheese

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Bread 2 slices
Butter 1 tablespoon
cheese 2 slices

Step 1: Preheat your skillet or frying pan: Place it on the stove over medium-low heat while you prepare your sandwich.
Step 2: Butter the bread: Take one slice of bread and spread butter on one side. This will be the outside of your sandwich.
Step 3: Add cheese: Place the cheese slices on the unbuttered side of the bread. If you like, you can add other fillings like ham or tomato slices at this point.
Step 4: Assemble the sandwich: Place the other slice of bread on top of the cheese, with the buttered side facing outwards.
Step 5: Grill the sandwich: Carefully place the sandwich onto the preheated skillet or frying pan. Cook until the bread becomes golden brown and crispy, and the cheese melts. This usually takes about 2-4 minutes per side, depending on your stove’s heat and the thickness of your bread.
Step 6: Flip and cook the other side: Once one side is golden brown, flip the sandwich over with a spatula and cook until the other side is also golden brown and the cheese is completely melted.
Step 7: Serve hot: Remove from the pan, cut if desired, and serve immediately while hot and gooey!

Recipe #2: Cookie

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 5 people
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 2.5 cups
sugar 1 cup
eggs 2 large eggs
vanilla extract 1 tsp
baking soda 1 tsp
chocolate chips 2 cups
  1. Preheat oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. Cream butter and sugars: In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until smooth and fluffy.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla: Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract.
  4. Combine dry ingredients: In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Mix dough: Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture, mixing until just combined. If using, stir in the chocolate chips.
  6. Form cookies: Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto the prepared baking sheets, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
  7. Bake cookies: Bake in the preheated oven for 10 to 12 minutes, or until the edges are lightly browned. The centers may still look slightly underdone, which will continue to set as they cool.

Recipe #3

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
salad greens 6 cups
feta cheese 1/4 cup
extra virgin olive oil 2 tbsp
balsamic vinegar 1 tbsp
cherry tomatoes 1 cup
cucumber whole, sliced

Step 1: Prepare vegetables: Wash and chop salad greens, halve cherry tomatoes, slice cucumber, and thinly slice red onion.
Step 2: Assemble salad: In a large bowl, combine salad greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion. Sprinkle with crumbled feta cheese if using.
Step 3: Dress salad: Drizzle olive oil and balsamic vinegar over the salad. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
Step 4: Toss and serve: Gently toss everything together until well combined. Serve immediately.
